# BLUFF - Quick Start Guide

## Instant Play (3 Steps)

### 1. Create a Room
- Click **"CREATE ROOM"** on the landing page
- Enter your name
- Choose number of players (3-8)
- Toggle bot slots if you want AI opponents
- Click **"CREATE ROOM"**

### 2. Invite Friends (Optional)
- Copy the room code (e.g., "KQ7X2J")
- Share the link or QR code
- Friends join by entering the code or clicking the link

### 3. Start Playing
- Host clicks **"START GAME"** when ready
- Cards are dealt automatically
- Play your turn when highlighted in gold!

## How to Play Your Turn

### Playing Cards
1. Click cards in your hand to select them (they lift up with a gold border)
2. Click **"PLAY SELECTED (N)"**
3. Choose what rank to declare
4. Cards are placed face-down on the pile

### Calling Bluff
- Think someone is lying? Click **"CALL BLUFF 🔥"**
- Cards are revealed
- If they lied: They pick up the pile
- If they were honest: You pick up the pile

### Passing
- Don't want to play? Click **"PASS"**
- (Not available if you started the round)

## Winning Strategy Tips

1. **Be Strategic**: You can play ANY cards and declare them as the active rank
2. **Watch the Count**: If someone claims 3 Kings but you have 2 Kings, they might be bluffing!
3. **Timing**: Sometimes it's better to pass than risk picking up a large pile
4. **First to Empty**: Get rid of all your cards to win!

## Playing with Bots

Bots are smart opponents that:
- Play strategically (70% honest when they have the cards)
- Call bluffs based on probability
- Think for 1.5-2.5 seconds to feel realistic

Perfect for testing or playing solo!

## Game Rules Reminder

### Round Flow
1. First player picks a rank (e.g., "Kings")
2. Everyone must play that rank for the round
3. You can lie about what you're playing!
4. Call bluff to catch liars
5. Loser picks up pile and starts new round

### Win Condition
**First player to empty their hand wins!**

## Troubleshooting

### "Room not found"
- Check the room code is correct (6 characters)
- Room may have expired or been deleted

### "Room is full"
- The room has reached max players
- Create a new room instead

### Can't start game
- Need minimum 3 players (human + bots)
- Must be the room host

### Game feels slow
- Check your internet connection
- Game updates every second
- Bot turns have a realistic delay (1.5-2.5s)

## Sound Setup (Optional)

The game has placeholder audio. To add sounds:
1. Place .mp3 files in `/public/sounds/`
2. Use the names from `/public/sounds/README.md`
3. Refresh the game

Game works fine without audio!
